#Slide: 4
#Header: What are Derivatives?
#Content:
- Derivatives represent the rate of change of a function at any given point
- They provide information about the slope or tangent line to the function
- Used to analyze and understand the behavior of functions

#Slide: 5
#Header: Applications of Derivatives
#Content:
- Calculating velocity and acceleration
- Finding maximum and minimum values
- Solving optimization problems
- Analyzing growth and decay
- Understanding the shape of curves

#Slide: 6
#Header: Notation and Terminology
#Content:
- Derivative notation: f'(x), dy/dx, d/dx(f(x))
- Terminology: slope, tangent line, secant line, local maximum/minimum, concavity

#Slide: 7
#Header: Derivative of a Function
#Content:
- Finding the derivative of a function using rules and formulas
- The power rule, product rule, chain rule, and quotient rule

#Slide: 8
#Header: Rules of Differentiation
#Content:
- Power rule: (x^n)' = nx^(n-1)
- Product rule: (f(x)g(x))' = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)
- Chain rule: (f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x))g'(x)
- Quotient rule: (f(x)/g(x))' = (f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x))/(g(x))^2
